Forgot your master password?

Kit can't recover your master password — by design, only you ever knew it. An admin in your workspace can reset your vault so you can pick a new one.

Your stored secrets are not lost. They're protected by a workspace key admins can re-share with you after the reset.

What happens next:

  1. You send the reset request below.
  2. Every workspace admin sees a card asking them to approve.
  3. An admin verifies it's really you (Slack DM, in person, etc.) and clicks Approve.
  4. Your old vault registration is wiped. You'll see a card on your stack with a link to set a new master password.
  5. After you save, an admin re-grants your access.
