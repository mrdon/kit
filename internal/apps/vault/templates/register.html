Set up your vault

The vault is where your team stores shared secrets — API keys, service credentials, anything you'd otherwise paste into a wiki or DM.

It's end-to-end encrypted: even Kit can't read what's inside. That's why you're picking a master password here, separate from your Slack login. It only ever lives in your head and on this device — Kit never sees it.

If you lose it, Kit can't recover it. But your teammates can: an admin resets your registration, you pick a new password, and they re-grant your access. Stored secrets are never lost.

At least 14 characters. Kit cannot recover it — only your teammates can re-grant access if you forget.
